Our Story

A Firm Built on Principle, Grown by Excellence

Adeyemi & Partners was founded in Abuja in 1998 by Chief Chukwuemeka Adeyemi, Senior Advocate of Nigeria. Beginning as a boutique practice of four attorneys focused on corporate advisory, the firm was built on a single unshakeable conviction: that Nigerian businesses both large and small deserved access to legal counsel of the highest international standard, delivered with deep local knowledge and genuine care for the client's outcome.

Over the following 25 years, the firm grew steadily and purposefully. We expanded our practice to encompass mergers & acquisitions, energy & resources, dispute resolution, securities law, real estate, and banking & finance. Always led by partners who are recognised authorities in their fields. Today, with offices in Abuja, Lagos, and London, and a team of over 48 legal professionals, Adeyemi & Partners is ranked among Nigeria's premier corporate law firms by The Legal 500 and Chambers & Partners.

We have advised on transactions worth over ₦2 trillion in aggregate, represented clients in landmark Supreme Court and International Court of Arbitration proceedings, and served as trusted counsel to Fortune 500 companies entering the Nigerian market, indigenous conglomerates restructuring for growth, and high-net-worth individuals navigating complex estates and investments.
